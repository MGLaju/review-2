About A .R. Rahman

Music director and Singer

A.R. Rahman, born on January 6, 1967, is an Indian composer, singer, and music producer. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest music composers in the Indian film industry and has also gained international recognition. Rahman's unique blend of Indian classical music and electronic sounds has earned him numerous awards, including two Academy Awards and two Grammy Awards. He has composed music for a wide range of films and his work has touched the hearts of millions around the world. Rahman continues to inspire with his mesmerizing melodies and innovative approach to music.
